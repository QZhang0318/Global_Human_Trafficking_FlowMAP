Human Trafficking Flow Dashboard

Our Goal

Our team—Carmen Liang, Qiong Zhang, and Sarah Farooq—created this page and its visualizations to shine light on the global patterns of human trafficking. Our goal is to make the data more accessible and actionable for those working to prevent exploitation around the world.

Our Audience

This page is intended for researchers, nonprofits, policy makers, journalists, and educators interested in understanding and combating human trafficking. Use the charts to explore demographic trends and follow the flow map to see how many victims move across the world.

The Data

All visualizations are based on the Global Synthetic Dataset from the Counter-Trafficking Data Collaborative (CTDC). This dataset combines real-world patterns with synthetic data to protect sensitive information while revealing critical insights about trafficking trends from 2002 to 2023.
